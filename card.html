Humpback Whale

Adult females are slightly larger than adult males by about 10-11%.
  • Scientific Name:Megaptera novaeangliae
  • Average Length: 39 to 52 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 45-50 years
  • Habitat: along the coasts of all oceans

With thirty broad ventral grooves on the throat and chest, along with it narrow flippers humpback whales can be distingush from other baleen whales. These gentle giants usually live along the coasts of most oceans and have even been known to come close to shore.
